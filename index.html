The problem

Breeders should not face a huge subscription jump simply because they own more than a handful of animals.

HerdHarbor is being built differently.

The first release

Everything needed to replace notebooks and scattered spreadsheets.

The private beta will focus on the records breeders use every day, with room to expand into a complete homestead platform.

Why HerdHarbor

Your record system should become more useful as you grow—not dramatically more expensive.

HerdHarbor began after livestock and rabbit breeders voiced the same frustration: affordable plans often become unaffordable as soon as their operation grows.

We are building a modern, practical platform around transparent pricing, fast barn-side data entry, and the records real breeders already rely on.

No surprise price cliffs Growth should be a success, not a penalty.
Designed with real breeders Beta feedback will shape the product.
Built to expand Rabbit-first, with support for mixed livestock and homesteads.

Questions

What to expect.

Will HerdHarbor be only for rabbits?

No. Rabbits are the first launch focus because their breeding and litter workflows are an excellent test case. The system is being structured for poultry, goats, sheep, cattle, pigs, and mixed homesteads.

How much will it cost?

Final pricing has not been announced. The guiding promise is fair, transparent pricing without an extreme jump simply because the user has more animals.

Will I be able to import existing records?

Import and export are part of the product direction. The specific file formats will be determined from beta-user needs and the systems breeders currently use.

When will the app launch?

HerdHarbor will begin with a small private beta. Early-access members will receive progress updates and invitations as testing opens.
